Customer Churn Prediction using Machine Learning

Project Overview

Customer churn is one of the biggest challenges faced by telecom companies. This project predicts whether a customer is likely to leave the company based on customer demographics, service details, and billing information.

The project includes complete data analysis, visualization, machine learning model building, and business recommendations.

---

 Dataset

IBM Telco Customer Churn Dataset

Features include:

- Gender
- Senior Citizen
- Partner
- Dependents
- Contract
- Payment Method
- Internet Service
- Monthly Charges
- Total Charges
- Tenure
- Churn (Target)

---

Key Business Insights

- Month-to-month contract customers are more likely to churn.
- Customers with higher monthly charges show higher churn.
- Customers with lower tenure are at greater risk of leaving.
- Fiber Optic users have relatively higher churn.
- Electronic Check customers show higher churn rates.
